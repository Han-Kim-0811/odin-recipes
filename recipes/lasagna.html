Lasagna

Description

This is a lasagna recipe provided by the odin project.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna noodle
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce.
  2. Make cheese mix
  3. Cook lasagna noodles.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Bake the lasagna.
